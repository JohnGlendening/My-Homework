About Me

I have a pet chicken named Janet. Sometimes I feel as though Janet is scheming behind my back. I've noticed items in my backyard have been strategically moved to create road-blocks for maneuvering through the grass. I've also noticed Janet's strange habit of watching me at night from her perch upon my window sill. She never leaves that spot even in the worst of conditions. Janet plays coy when I deliver her food and other comfort items, but I know she's up to something. There's more to me than my friend Janet, but I'll not bore you with the details.
